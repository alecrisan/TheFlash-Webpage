The Flash

The Flash is an American superhero television series developed by Greg Berlanti, Andrew Kreisberg, and Geoff Johns, airing on The CW. It is based on the DC Comics character Barry Allen / Flash, a costumed superhero crime-fighter with the power to move at superhuman speeds.
 

Main Cast

  • Grant Gustin as Barry Allen/The Flash
  • Candice Patton as Iris West
  • Danielle Panabaker as Caitlin Snow/Killer Frost
  • Carlos Valdes as Cisco Ramon/Vibe
  • Rick Cosnett as Eddie Thawne
  • Tom Cavanagh as Eobard Thawne/Reverse-Flash, Harrison "Harry" Wells
  • Jesse L. Martin as Joe West

Crossovers

The Flash has been a part of several Crossovers that included other shows from the Arrowverse, such as Arrow, Supergirl and Legends Of Tomorrow.

But the one that everyone is waiting for is Crisis on Infinite Earths, which will be released this fall.

This all draws parallels with the original 'Crisis' story in DC Comics where Barry Allen sacrificed himself to save the entire universe from the Anti-Monitor. During this comic book crossover, Supergirl also died – in a scene that was deliberately echoed visually by the season two premiere of her own show after it moved to The CW.
